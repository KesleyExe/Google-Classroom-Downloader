# 📚 Classroom Quick Downloader
### *Never Lose Another Assignment Again*

---

## What Is This?

**Classroom Quick Downloader** is a one-click tool that downloads **every PDF, worksheet, slide deck, and document** from your Google Classroom courses—automatically organized into folders on your computer.

No more hunting through 47 announcements to find that one lab report from September. No more "the file is in the Drive somewhere" panic at 11 PM. Just run it, pick your courses, and everything lands on your desktop, neatly sorted.

---

## Why You Need This

| The Problem | The Solution |
|-------------|--------------|
| Teachers post materials across announcements, assignments, and coursework tabs | **One scan catches everything** |
| Google Docs disappear when you're offline | **Converts everything to PDFs & Office files** |
| Course materials get buried under new posts | **Downloads your entire course history** |
| Studying for exams means clicking through 200 posts | **Everything is local, searchable, and organized** |
| Group project files are scattered across Drive | **One folder per course, one folder per lesson** |

---

## What It Does

- **🔍 Scans your entire classroom** — announcements, assignments, coursework materials, and attached files
- **📄 Converts Google Docs/Sheets/Slides** to PDF, Excel, and PowerPoint formats you can actually use offline
- **📁 Organizes everything automatically** — course name → lesson name → files
- **🔗 Saves links too** — YouTube videos and web links saved as `.url` shortcuts
- **⚡ Resumes interrupted downloads** — no need to start over if your Wi-Fi drops
- **🔒 Works entirely on your computer** — your data never touches a third-party server

---

## How It Works (The 30-Second Version)

1. **Sign in** with your Google account (same login you use for Classroom)
2. **Pick your courses** from the list — type `all` if you want everything
3. **Grab a coffee** — the tool scans and downloads while you wait
4. **Find everything** in `C:\Users\You\ClassroomDownloads\`

---

## What Gets Downloaded

| From Classroom | What You Get |
|----------------|--------------|
| Google Docs | `.pdf` files |
| Google Sheets | `.xlsx` Excel files |
| Google Slides | `.pptx` PowerPoint files |
| PDFs, images, videos | Original files, unchanged |
| YouTube links | `.url` shortcuts |
| Web links | `.url` shortcuts |
| Announcements with attachments | Organized in `_Announcements` folder |
